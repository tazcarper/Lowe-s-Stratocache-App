Frequently asked questions.

What is Home Automation or Smart Home?

The use of the Internet to automate and control the devices in your home for improved comfort, energy efficiency and security.

What is Iris?

Iris is a simple, customizable home automation system by Lowe's. Iris lets you manage your smart devices from a single app, giving you control, whether you’re home or away. Great for home owners or renters!

Why should I buy Iris over other systems?

  • Iris by Lowe’s offers a wide breadth of products with over 50 devices.
  • Iris is FREE for basic use and has a premium service as well to create rules between devices. (turn on your lights when the motion detector senses movement)
  • DIY – Create the system you want, grow it over time.
  • No long-term commitments with month-to-month service.
  • Value priced products from nationally recognized brands.
  • Iris devices are wireless which allows for easy portability.

What do I need?

  • Iris Hub
  • Smartphone
  • Broadband Internet
  • Router or modem
  • Iris compatible products

How do I get started?

Getting started is easy. Purchase an Iris hub, download the app and connect your products.
